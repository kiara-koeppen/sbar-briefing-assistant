# ED Locum Coverage — Status Update

**Period:** January 6 – February 14, 2026 (six weeks post-locum start)
**Prepared by:** Director of Imaging
**Distribution:** VP of Operations

## Locum Coverage Summary

Locum radiologist (3pm–11pm shift, weekdays + Saturdays) started January 6,
2026. Contract is in place through end of Q1 2026 ($180K total).

## ED CT Turnaround Time

| Metric | Q3 2025 (pre-locum) | Q4 2025 (gap period) | Jan 2026 | Feb 1–14 2026 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Median order-to-read | 38 min | 71 min | 44 min | 41 min |
| 90th percentile | 62 min | 118 min | 78 min | 71 min |
| Median scan-to-read | 19 min | 49 min | 24 min | 22 min |

The locum coverage has restored turnaround to within 8% of the Q3 baseline.

## ED Throughput

| Metric | Q3 2025 baseline | Oct/Nov/Dec 2025 | Jan 2026 | Feb 1–14 2026 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Door-to-disposition | 4.7 hrs | 5.7 hrs | 5.0 hrs | 4.8 hrs |
| LWBS rate | 3.6% | 4.9% | 3.4% | 3.1% |
| ED volume | 28,400 | 32,600 (annualized) | 2,940 | 1,420 (period) |

LWBS has recovered below the 3.6% Q3 figure. Door-to-disposition is approaching
the original 4.0 hr target.

## Patient Satisfaction

Press Ganey ED domain has recovered 14 percentile points from the November low
(was -18 from Q2 baseline; currently -4 from Q2 baseline).

## Locum Performance Notes

- Read quality: no significant variance from internal radiologist reads
  (audited via 50-case review by the Quality team).
- Schedule reliability: zero shift cancellations in the 6-week period.
- Cost: tracking on budget at $30K/month against the $180K total.
